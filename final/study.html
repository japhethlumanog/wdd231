Study Tips

How to Study Effectively

Set a Consistent Time

Pick a time each day — morning is ideal. Even 10 minutes builds a lasting habit.

Write It Down

Keep a journal of impressions and insights you receive while reading.

Pray First

Ask Heavenly Father to help you understand and apply what you read.

Mark Key Verses

Highlight verses that stand out. Return to them often and share them with others.
